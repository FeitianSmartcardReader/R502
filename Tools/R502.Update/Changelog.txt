How to using Feitian_R502_Update to do upgrade fireware for R502:
1. Open Feitian_R502_Update
2. Choose config file:
	R502 CL(contactless reader) please choose  R502_Contactless_Config.ini
	R502 Dual interface, please choose R502_Dual_Interface_Config.ini
3. Click connect and select your BIN file, do update
